This example shows how to add canvas instructions before and after OL3 standard drawing phase. Click on plane icons to select them.

Pre- and post-render functions allows to "decorate" a polygon with pixel-based elements (for instance, additional text, hatching patterns, or other elements). As the feature itself is a parameter of the function, drawings can be based on feature properties.

Extension points on a Polygon are:

  • Before drawing the fill of the Polygon
  • After drawing the fill of the Polygon
  • Before drawing the stroke of the Polygon
  • After drawing the stroke of the Polygon

Beside pre- and post-render functions, there are also foreground-render functions, that are rendered once all other layers have been rendered. Z-order of layers and features are maintained while rendering foreground-render functions.

Note: elements added through pre- and post-render functions are not clickable.
